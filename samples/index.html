List of Samples

Vehicle Registration for Indian cities - dashboard

The data format has been taken from data.gov.in and provides a dashboard view of the registered motor vehicles in India from 2001 to 2011. The dashboard is looking at cities which have more than a million vehicle registrations.This uses melt.js, crossfilter,d3 and dc for the visualization.

US population density by state capitals

This uses the DC.js choropleth charts to map US state wise population with color coding of red to green to indicate the lower to high density of population. This has been inspired by the wonderful sample provided for US Venture Capital Landscape 2011 (http://dc-js.github.io/dc.js/vc/)

Live Earth quake - dashboard

Live visualization of data from earthquake-report.com updated every 10 minutes

Indian Railway Zones and Divisions

D3 tree map to represent the India Railway Zones & Division hierarchy
